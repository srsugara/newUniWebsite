VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES
  • Children’s Program Volunteer
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=1105
    Play with children/teens at the Center for Women in Transition Tuesdays and Thursdays. Though children can be as old as 18, they can be as young as newborn, so high school volunteers will probably be welcome. A 30-60 min. interview is required, and you must be 16 to volunteer. Training January 18, 2005.
  • Assist Non-Profit with Websites
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=176
    Prairienet is always looking for experienced, community-minded Webheads and graphic designers to work on projects for local community service organizations. If interested, ask Ariel to see the email prairienet sent me with their concerns (I think they underestimate your maturity).
  • Web Graphic Design for Community Organizations
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=395
    Work with area community service organizations to provide graphic design for web sites. Must apply with sample of work. Again, ask Ariel for the email.
  • Cat Socializing
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=833
    Cuddle adoptable cats at the Champaign County Humane Society any day of the week. Application and Orientation/Training necessary.
  • Dog Walking
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=832
    Walk adoptable dogs at the Champaign County Humane Society any day of the week. Application and Orientation/Training necessary
  • Virginia Theatre Volunteer
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=1159
    All the joys of working at a theatre without getting paid ^_~ Great for those passionate in theatre, and shows are generally in the evening (good for us full-time students). Take tickets, work concession and usher, and see some free shows when the work is done. Must fill out application.
  • Champaign Park District
    http://www.champaignparkdistrict.com/volunt.htm
    Apply for the teen volunteer team. Must be 13 or older. Teen volunteers may work no more than 25 hours a week.
  • Champaign Public Library Volunteer
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=1338
    Shelf books any time the library is open.
  • The Reading Group general assistant
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=740
    “the perfect ‘after-school’ or weekend commitment for a youth interested in gaining volunteer experience.” Keep the Reading Group clean and tidy. Application/Interview process required.
  • Provena Covenant Medical Center
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=944
    Apply to volunteer during the summer session for Medical/Surgical Unit or Cardiac Services, Gift Shop Assistant, Engineering, Emergency Medical Services, or Senior Services/VIP Program. For high school Juniors and Seniors.
  • Early American Museum Junior Counselor
    http://www.cuvolunteer.org/find/opp_profile.phtml?oid=1432
    Looking for volunteers age 12-18 to help with summer and scout programs held 9am-noon on Wednesdays for younger children. Application required.
