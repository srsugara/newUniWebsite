Nationally Renowned High School
Uni High is a public laboratory school enrolling approximately 322 students. Founded in 1921, the 5-year high school is located on the University of Illinois campus in Urbana-Champaign.

Our Mission
University of Illinois Laboratory High School seeks to spark the creative fervor and high aspirations of talented young people, to inspire them to excellence, to challenge them through traditional and experimental strategies, to ignite their potential for active and responsible involvement in the adult world, and to positively influence the larger educational community.
