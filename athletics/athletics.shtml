Quick Facts:
  • Over 80% of our student body participates in our "No-Cut" athletics programs
  • Students can choose from 11 sports through 3 different seasons
  • Uni's Cross Country, Boys' Soccer, and Girls and Boys Track and Field are consistently recognized as some of the areas' best programs
  • In 2016, Uni High was honored with the "News-Gazette Athlete of the year" in Girls' Cross Country, Boys' Golf, and Girls' Swimming
  • Uni High is in the East Central Illinois Conference along with area school Judah Christian
  • In 2012 and 2016, the boys' soccer team finished second and fourth respectively at the IHSA 1A State Tournament
  • At the 2016 State Swimming Tournament, current junior Ema Rajic broke two state records
