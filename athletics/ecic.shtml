EAST CENTRAL ILLINOIS CONFERENCE (ECIC)

The East Central Illinois Conference member schools have formed this athletic conference based on equity of competition and proximity of location. All activities shall be conducted with the knowledge that we are a conference of different missions and we will always be respectful of our differences and will strive to maintain a comfortable atmosphere for all of our visitors. Communication is essential to the existence of any partnership therefore, the ECIC recognizes the importance of good communication between member schools.

The following are the sports offered by the East Central Illinois Conference:

Fall Sports

  • Boys Soccer
  • Girls Volleyball

Winter Sports

  • Boys Basketball
  • Girls Basketball

Spring Sports

  • Baseball
  • Girls Soccer
